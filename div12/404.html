Club Bleach Love Portal
Nicknames: IRC nicknames can be changed simply by typing /nick NEWNICKNAME into the text box. If you try to use a nickname that someone has already registered and yo do not know the password for it, the server will change your name within 60 seconds in most cases.
Other Channels: You can join another channel or create a new channel very easily by typing /join #NEWCHANNEL into the text box. You will still remain in the #ClubBleach channel, and you can switch between them simply by clicking on the appopriate channel tab. For example, if you wanted to create a "ClubBleachGFX" channel, you could type in /join #ClubBleachGFX . :)
Voice, HalfOp, Op, Admin, and Founder (+%@&~):
  • Voice (+): Voice simply means that if the Channel Operators "mute" the channel, people who have Voice can still type. Those without Voice (or above) will become "muted."
  • HalfOp (%): HalfOps include our Club Bleach Moderators. They have the ability to kick people from the channel, among other things.
  • Ops (@): Ops include our Bleach Portal staff and our Club Bleach SuperMods. They can kick, ban, change the channel topic, and more.
  • Admin/Protected (&): Admins are our Club Bleach Admins. They cannot be kicked from the channel by anyone except the Founder, and have all the abilities of Ops.
  • Founder (~): The Founder is Sarteck.
